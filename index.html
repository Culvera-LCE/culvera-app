Validate
Upload your compliant .txt. AI checks structure, activity descriptions, and product-line compliance. Fix and resubmit until all checks pass.

Confirm
One click creates the Drive folder tree, writes register rows, seeds the pipeline queue, and places the A01 gate file — ready for generation.

Courses
Live dashboard of all courses and activities. See generation status, approval state, and 90-day refresh countdowns across every product line.

Review & Manage
